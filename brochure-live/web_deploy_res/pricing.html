Engineering Support & FAQ

How does Vehicle Lab ensure ISO 26262 compliance?

expand_more

Our HELIX and DiagAI suites are developed using a safety-lifecycle compliant process. We provide Tool Qualification Data Kits (TQDK) that assist your safety engineers in justifying the use of Vehicle Lab in ASIL-certified development projects.

Can we integrate custom AI models into DiagAI?

expand_more

Yes, DiagAI supports Python-based agent definitions. You can upload your own TensorFlow or PyTorch models into our sandbox, where they can interact with real-time CAN/LIN bus data via our standard API hooks.
