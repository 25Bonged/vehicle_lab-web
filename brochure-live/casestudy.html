Challenge: Engineers spend hours manually verifying if test cycles meet strict EU WLTP/RDE standards.

Solution: The WLTPAgent automatically validates velocity profiles, soak times, and ambient conditions against the standard.

Result: Instant "Pass/Fail" reports generated immediately after test completion. Zero manual calculation errors.
